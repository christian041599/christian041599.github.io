About me

Who am I?

I’m someone that loves having tons of brain stimulation. Video games, puzzles, or brain teasers. Whatever it is, as long as it keeps the cogs in my brain turning my attention will be fully focused and locked into that activity. I’ve been told I’m a very sociable person and someone many people can call a friend. I grew up here in St. Louis until the age of 3 when my family and I moved to Portland, Maine. After freezing to death for the next 5 years, we came back here to be closer to family. I’ve lived in St. Louis ever since and mostly enjoyed my stay.
